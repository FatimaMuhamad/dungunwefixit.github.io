What is a Smartwatch? 


A smartwatch is a wearable computing device that closely resembles a wristwatch or other time-keeping device. Aside smartphones, smartwatch also becomes an essential to most people nowadays. The smartwatch can tell you a lot of things, including what your heart rate, answer phone calls from your mobile phone, read email and messages, listen to music, get daily weather report and ask a digital assistant a question.
